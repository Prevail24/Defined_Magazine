WE ARE ACCEPTING SUBMISSIONS!

Submissions must follow the guidelines below:

  • Title for the editorial
  • Photographer
  • Model
  • MUA & Hair
  • Wardrobe

PHOTOS Must Be:

  • Vertical Photographs - 8x10 minumum
  • Horizontal Photographs - 17x11 minimum
  • 300-350dpi

Please send 3-8 images (no photo will be accepted with a visible watermark). Include the edition you are submitting for in the subject line of the email along with the photographer's name.

Upon publication, tearsheets will be made available via Dropbox for download, and copies of the magazines will be available, with a link added showing the location for purchase.
